Book Recommendations

  • The Palace Of Illusions

    by Chitra Banerjee Divakaruni

    A rendition of the Hindu epic Mahabharata as told from Draupadi's viewpoint.

  • The Alchemist

    by Paulo Coelho

    A Spanish shepherd boy travels to Egypt in search of treasure and his Personal Legend.

  • Carrie

    by Stephen King

    A bullied high-school girl discovers she has telekinetic powers.

  • Gone Girl

    by Gillian Flynn

    A psychological thriller that examines the dynamics of marriage and lies.

  • Hyperion

    by Dan Simmons

    A science fiction novel featuring seven pilgrims on a mysterious journey.

  • Happy Place

    by Emily Henry

    A couple discovers along the way what finding your “happy place” really means.
